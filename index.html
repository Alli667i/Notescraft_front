Common Questions

Do I have to wait for my PDF to process?

Nope! Upload your file and close the tab. We'll send you an email when your notes are ready (usually 5-10 minutes). Your file is saved securely for 7 days, so you can download it whenever you're ready.

How is this different from ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is incredible for brainstorming and answering questions. But when it comes to turning a 20-page PDF into structured notes, it takes 20-30 minutes of prompting, regenerating, and formatting. We built NotesCraft to do that specific job in one click ‚Äî so you can get back to studying faster.

Will important details get lost?

No. We extract all key concepts and context ‚Äî nothing critical is skipped. We reorganize the structure to make it easier to study, but we don't cut corners like AI summarizers do.

How long does it take?

Most PDFs process in 5-10 minutes. You'll see real-time progress updates and get a download link when it's ready.

What format do I get?

A clean, editable Word document (.docx) with headings, bullet points, and proper structure. You can highlight, edit, and share it like any other document.

Is my data safe?

Yes. We process your PDF using secure servers and delete it immediately after generating your notes. We don't store or share your files.

How many PDFs can I process?

Free accounts get 3 PDFs per day (up to 25 pages each). That's enough for most students' daily workload.
